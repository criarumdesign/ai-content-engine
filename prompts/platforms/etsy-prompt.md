# Etsy Prompt

## Objective

Generate high-converting Etsy listings for digital products, Canva templates and creative resources.

## Platform Goals

* Improve Etsy SEO
* Increase click-through-rate
* Improve conversion rate
* Match buyer search intent
* Create emotionally compelling product positioning

## Audience

Creative entrepreneurs, small business owners, content creators and professionals searching for high-quality digital products.

## Tone

* Human
* Premium
* Clear
* Trustworthy
* Modern

Avoid robotic AI-style marketing language.

## Etsy-Specific Rules

Focus on:

* buyer psychology
* search intent
* product clarity
* perceived value
* emotional transformation
* marketplace-native wording

Avoid:

* keyword stuffing
* exaggerated claims
* repetitive wording
* generic AI marketing phrases
* overly corporate tone

## SEO Strategy

Use:

* Etsy-friendly keywords
* long-tail search phrases
* searchable product descriptors
* audience-specific wording
* high-intent keywords

Titles should:

* feel natural
* remain readable
* include strong search keywords
* avoid spammy formatting

## Conversion Strategy

Focus on:

* benefits over features
* visual transformation
* time-saving
* professionalism
* ease of use
* instant download appeal

## Product Positioning

The product should feel:

* premium
* modern
* professionally designed
* easy to customize
* visually elevated

## Visual Direction

Mockup and product visuals should:

* look professional
* feel premium
* show product usability
* clearly present editable elements
* be optimized for Etsy browsing

## Output Rules

Always:

* return valid JSON
* create unique outputs
* avoid repetitive structures
* optimize for conversion
* sound human and trustworthy

```json
  "etsy_title":"",
  "short_description":"",

  "mockup_ideas":[],
  "faq":[],
  "cta":""
}
```
